ABOUT US

We are a software company based in London. Our job is understanding legacy software, and making it live longer and better. Also, we are happy addressing robust and validated technologies, with their pros and cons. When a technology lasts many years, there might be a good reason for that.

Our clients have invested massively into their information systems. Keeping these systems fit and running is an absolute priority.
