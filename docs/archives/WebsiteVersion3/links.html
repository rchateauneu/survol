Documentation

Exploring LegacyWare with Survol is a  talk, organised by Eugene Morozov of Semantic Web London and hosted by Lab49. Slides are stored on SlideShare and a video is available on Youtube.

DMTF-CIM to OWL: A Case Study in Ontology Conversion: This research article from Dennis Heimbigner of the Computer Science Department of the University of Colorado Boulder, describes the process of translating a software engineering ontology expressed from the Distributed Management Task Force (DMTF) Common Information Model (CIM) into the W3C OWL-DL (Ontology Web Language Description Logics) ontology language.
